## 👨‍💻 About Me

Welcome to my piece of the internet! I'm a student at **The University of Texas at Austin** studying Computer Science and Mathematics (GPA: 3.91). I am deeply passionate about the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Systems, and Software Engineering, constantly seeking new ways to model applications and predict system bottlenecks.

- 🔭 **Currently:** Conducting research at the **Learning Directed Operating System (LDOS)** lab where I'm developing dynamic microservice schedulers using Bayesian optimization and graph application embeddings. Also serving as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for **CS 331 (Algorithms & Complexity)** with Dr. Fares Fraij.
- 🌱 **Previously:** Developed transformer-based motion trajectory models at the **Living With Robots Laboratory (LWR)** for robotic social navigation, beating state-of-the-art STPOTR error rates by over 50%.
- 💼 **Next Stop:** Joining **Google** as a Software Engineering Intern in NYC (Summer 2026) to work in the Engineering Productivity organization in Google Cloud Platform, automating internal Java fuzz testing via Google ADK.
- 🏆 **Awards:** "Best Use of Google Cloud" @ HackTX 2023 | 5x University Honors
