## 👨‍💻 About Me

Welcome to my piece of the internet! I'm a student at **The University of Texas at Austin** studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I'm deeply passionate about the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Systems, and Software Engineering. 

- 🔭 **Currently:** Conducting research at the **Learning Directed Operating System (LDOS)** lab where I'm developing dynamic microservice schedulers using Bayesian optimization and graph application embeddings.
- 🌱 **Previously:** Developed transformer-based motion trajectory models at the **Living With Robots Laboratory (LWR)** for robotic social navigation.
- 💼 **Next Stop:** Joining **Google** as a Software Engineering Intern in NYC (Summer 2026) to build AI-powered fuzz testing tools.
- 🏫 **Additionally:** Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for CS 331 (Algorithms & Complexity).
- 🏆 **Awards:** "Best Use of Google Cloud" @ HackTX 2023 | 5x University Honors
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I love creating high-performance personal portfolios with custom cyberpunk neon aesthetics. Check out my latest version!
